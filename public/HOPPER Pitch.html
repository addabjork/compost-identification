04 — The Idea HOPPER

Put the intelligence inside the bin.

An item drops in
→
Camera classifies it
→
Running score per bin
  • An IR camera at the opening reads each item — no images leave the device.
  • One item is ~75% accurate; hundreds of items make the bin score reliable.
Score = compostable events ÷ classifiable events 04
05 — From Score To Route HOPPER

The score decides where it goes.

Routing threshold
≥ 60% → MBT / Organics
< 60% → Standard refuse

High-scoring bins cluster geographically — so routing happens by zone, not one can at a time.

06 — The Shift HOPPER

We stop fixing the person.
We fix the infrastructure.

Today

The human sorts.

Education, mandates, fines — and still ~4–20% capture. The burden sits with every resident, every day.

With HOPPER

The bin sorts.

The camera and the route do the work. It happens at the can, every time — nothing to teach, nothing to remember.

A street stream that's 100% landfill today becomes 30–50% recovered — with zero behavior change.

07 — Roadmap HOPPER

Prove it small. Scale it where the data says.

Phase 1 · Year 1–2
Collect data

Retrofit cameras into 50–100 Better Bins across 3 high-traffic zones. Score only — no routing yet.

Phase 2 · Year 2–3
Route a zone

Run a dedicated organics truck in the highest-scoring zone. Measure diversion and cost-per-ton.

Phase 3 · Year 3–5
Scale + MBT

Embed cameras in new bin runs; contract or co-invest in MBT capacity if the economics hold.
